Vadapav



What's this?

Vada Pav is a savory and spicy soft dinner rolls or fluffy buns, referred to as Pav, stuffed with a fried batter coated potato dumpling fritter called Batata Vada and laced with spicy and sweet chutneys.


Ingredients:

for Vada:

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion
  • green chilli
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Curry leaves
  • Gram flour (Besan)
  • Water and oil

pav and extras:

  • pav buns
  • green chutney
  • dry garlic chutney

Recipe:

  1. Vada filling: Heat oil → mustard seeds → curry leaves → onion → ginger-garlic → chilli → turmeric → mashed potatoes → salt. Mix & cool.
  2. Shape vadas
  3. Batter: Mix besan + water + salt (thick).Batter: Mix besan + water + salt (thick).
  4. Fry: Dip vadas in batter → deep fry till golden.
  5. Assemble: Slice pav → chutneys → vada → press & enjoy !
