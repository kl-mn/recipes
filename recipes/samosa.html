Samosa



What's this?

Samosa is a crispy, golden-fried North Indian snack with a flaky pastry shell filled with a savory, spiced, tangy mixture of mashed potatoes, green peas, and aromatic spices.


Ingredients:

for Dough:

  • all-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Oil/ghee
  • carom seeds (optional)
  • Salt
  • Water

for Filling:

  • potatoes (boiled and peeled)
  • green peas
  • oil
  • cumin seeds
  • grated ginger
  • green chillies (chopped)
  • red chilli powder
  • garam masala
  • salt
  • fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • lemon juice (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Make Dough: Mix flour, salt, and oil with a little water to create a firm dough; let it rest.
  2. Prepare Filling: Saut√© boiled potatoes, peas,green chillies,ginger and spices in a pan; let the mixture cool down.
  3. Shape: Roll dough into an oval, cut it in half, and form each piece into a cone shape.
  4. Fill and seal: Spoon the cooled potato mixture into the cone and pinch the edges tightly to seal the triangle.
  5. Fry: Deep-fry the samosas over a low flame until they are golden brown and crispy.
